Without you

Friday, June 25, 2010 3:48:49 AM

On the thinking about Julia Gillard being the first female PM ever of Australia, I was back to the old question that is, why women usually have to sacrifice their family life for a really high position in society, whereas it is so much easier for men to have both.

Then I came with the answer: Women are capable of looking after the family all by themselves in the absence of their husbands' support, while men simply just do not have that ability.
