Dan Brown and his smart wit

Wednesday, September 16, 2009 4:16:25 AM



Very first note: This is not the Dan Brown who wrote "Da Vinci Code"

Last week, when I was struggling with the research for my Opinion Piece assignment, I accidentally found Dan Brown's series about China on YouTube. The videos were so interesting and funny that once I got to watch them, I just couldn't stop but kept pressing to open other clips.

Dan Brown turns out be an ordinary boy (likes pogo stick and collects ... plastic spoons ) who enjoys vlogging and posts regular videos about his everyday life, his random thoughts, or his crazy ideas. Those China clips were produced when he had his trip to China for a cultural exchange program of his university, considered his first trip ever overseas. It is always interesting to see how Western people see and experience "The East", but Dan showed it in a damn funny way of his. One vlog about his opinion of "China smells funny", some about his battle with a mosquito in the room, others about Chinese snack that he bought in Walmart, which he had no clue what it was...

God, I just couldn't help laughing out loud when seeing those videos. And it's not funny in a cheesy way, it's just like we are blogging, he blogs by posting videos on YouTube. After watching all 11 videos about his China trip, I pressed for more related vids and came up with his own channel. Man it's just massive. Turned out that this guy has been vlogging for years, and is one of the most popular one on YouTube (last time I saw he was celebrating his 100,000th subscriber, not sure about now). He vlogs about everything, from his crazy idea of mixing everything in a McDonalds meal to make a new ... smoothie (which turned out to be horribly awful) to politics in the US (he's a Politics student), from how to solve a rubic cube (which I believe were his very first videos on the Tube) to the issue of abortion, from his thought of Harry Potter to Jonas Brothers. Or even sometimes til the end of the video, you just roll on the floor laughing and still don't know what the vid's about. But ... whatever it is, it is just crazily funny!!! (Guarantee!!! )

This guy even got his own live shows on the net every week. And what's most amazing is he can make money from that as well , simple, YouTube puts ads on his videos and he got a share of it. Wow ... he is just same age as me but paying for his study, and even more important, just by sharing his thoughts. Well ... can't deny that his videos are enticing, anyway!

Hmm ... feeling a little bit ashamed when I'm also 19 but have done nothing for the world, oh even for family. But at least I still got a consolation that ... I've been to more countries than him

Now just take my words and go watch his clips, I swear you won't regret

That'll make your day
